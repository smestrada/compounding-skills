## How the Operator View uses this

The skill compares your inbox activity against these priorities. If your inbox
is dominated by content unrelated to your focus areas, it will flag that as
strategic drift — with specific evidence from the day's emails.

This isn't about judgment. It's a signal that your attention may be leaking
somewhere you didn't intend.
